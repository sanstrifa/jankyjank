- 2023-02-19 | The World of Alteration

It's not my goal to be better than everyone around me and all egotistical. I want to help people understand some of the realities that are being manipulated by information distributors to take your time, money, and self-consciousness away. It is important for me to actually practice what I talk and complain about almost daily, either with people or with myself in my own mind. I dislike hypocrisy, and in order for me to be the change I want to see, I have to actuallybe the change the world needs. Daily reminders about who and what I am and the beliefs I hold. To question myself deeply about my thoughts and the things I am saying to other people. I am not perfect, in my opinion perfection does not truly exist I will say however that striving to be a better version of yourself and never stopping with some small vision of perfection is helpful. I say this because I beleive that if a person was to reach "Perfection" their mission would be complete and progress would sieze to a hault, and since perfect does not really exist progress is continuous. Change is inevitable, it cannot be stopped but it can be slowed down or sped up. This is a powerful quote I read which helped me initiate change into my life.

Reading Meditations by Marcus Aurelius has helped me bundle some strong pieces of wisdom together and improve my overall method of navigating my mind and the world around me.
